FAQ

Here are some FAQs

Where are all the Hostile Mobs / Why is the Nether + End Disabled?

Hostile mobs do not spawn anywhere in the world (The PVP Arena is an Ecception) as we try to mimic the real world

The Nether/End are disabled due to their unrealistic aspect unfitting with the Core Gameplay of the server.  However, you can do /shop to obtain the materials you would obtain from a mob or in a different dimension

Why Should I Link my Discord? 

Discord Linking although optional is reccomended as certain custom coded plugins may need discord to notify you. also you do get a nice tag for linking.

Where do i find the Sell Prices of everything?

We offer that information in our discord! It is in a channel called #sell-prices !
