About

Welcome to TheCommunityEarth server! TheCommunityEarth is a world server with towny and custom plugins on it. You can play on it with your friends or make new ones! Go to war for resources or do one of many events/quests on TCE. Enjoy your experience on The Community Earth!
IP : thecommunity.earth , version 1.12.2

Getting Started

TheCommunityEarth is a Earth sandbox server with a 1:1000 earth map. This guide will get you started on your adventure. Join using the address thecommunity.earth , join our Discord Server and open the Server Map in your browser. Using the server map, you can view where all the nations are and where there are free plots. You can obtain Money through many means such as quests, selling things off and more!. You can deposit this money into your town bank for town expenses such as claiming.
