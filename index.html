About Dazai

Dazai is a multipurpose bot providing many different features such as levelling, high quality music streaming, utility commands and more! 

Features

Some of my basic features:

Permissions

Knowing some commands may be too dangerous to grant to certain roles, you can easily configure which permissions go to which roles with an inheritance based permissions system!

Customizable & Convenience 

With a beautiful UI Web Panel, you can customize me with ease and Convenience or if you would like through old fashioned text commands on Discord.

Music

Think embeds look overused? worry no more! On top of providing only the highest quality music possible, We also generate stylish cards to provide a better music experience.

Moderation tools

On top of the regular moderation tools, I also provide novel tools like AI powered Moderation and language detection to assist in moderation of your servers!
